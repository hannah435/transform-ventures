Meet the founder.

One of the most recognized figures in blockchain and cryptocurrency — early bitcoin investor, author, and the entrepreneur CNBC called the "Godfather of Crypto."

Michael Terpin

Founder & CEO, Transform Ventures · CEO & CIO, Bitcoin Supercycle Fund

Early bitcoin investor, thought leader, and serial entrepreneur — known as the "Godfather of Crypto" (CNBC). Chief Investment Officer of the Bitcoin Supercycle Fund and author of Bitcoin Supercycle (Skyhorse Publishing, 2024), which correctly predicted the early November 2024 all-time high for bitcoin. Developed the "Four Seasons of Bitcoin" cycle model in 2015.

Minority General Partner of Alpha Transform Holdings (multi-strategy), Tradecraft Capital (liquid VC), and Sigma Capital (crypto VC). Co-founder of BitAngels (2013) and BitAngels Fund 1 (2014), the first crypto angel group. Creator of Tokenize, the leading conference series connecting investors with crypto. Previously founded Marketwire, the first Internet-based newswire (backed by Sequoia Capital), sold to NASDAQ for $200M.
